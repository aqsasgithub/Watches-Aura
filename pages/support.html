Frequently Asked Questions

What types of watches and clocks does Watches Aura offer?

Watches Aura offers a variety of watches and clocks, including sport, digital, classic, and minimalist designs and many more.

Can I find a specific watch or clock model on your website?

Use the search bar or browse through our categorized collections under the “Products” menu.

Do you offer warranties on your watches and clocks?

Yes, we offer a limited warranty on most of our products. Please refer to the product page for specific warranty details.

How do I register my watch for warranty coverage?

Register your watch by filling out the warranty form on our website under the “Support” section.

What is the return policy for Watches Aura products?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. The product must be in its original condition and packaging.

How can I adjust the band on my watch?

Many bands can be adjusted by removing or adding links. For assistance, visit a local jeweler or our store.

Are your watches water-resistant or waterproof?

Most of our watches are water-resistant; please check the product specifications for details on water resistance.

What materials are used in Watches Aura timepieces?

We use high-quality materials, including stainless steel, leather, and durable polymers, depending on the model.
